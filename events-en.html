Planning Accessible Events / Meetings

In order to assist you in offering a fully accessible event (virtual or in person), weather it is a training session, a meeting, a kiosk or an open door event, the IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) has established a list of things for you to do.

To do list – when relevant

  • Reference material (including the agenda) must be sent ahead of time (if possible two weeks ahead; or a minimum of two days ahead); must be in an accessible format and using plain language; must be available in English and in French in two separate documents.
  • In your invitation, ask if anyone requires an accommodation (for example a sign language interpreter or Communication Access Real-Time-CART) unless you know your participants.
  • Reservation for some of the accommodations may require up to three weeks ahead of time.
  • Prepare meeting budget to include the accommodation services cost.
  • Send all reference material with the invitation.
  • If you do not know who exactly will participate, then have accommodations available.
